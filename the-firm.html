The Firm & Our Partners

The Firm

Finsurety Advisors LLP which stands for ‘Financial Surety & Security’ for our clients is what we as a firm aim at achieving. Co-founded by two Chartered Accountants, Finsurety Advisors seeks to provide a one stop solution for clients looking for a diverse range of professional services. Along with our team of professionals from different domains we have managed to create a pool of individuals with rich experience in the fields of Debt Raising & Restructuring, Process Improvement, Risk Advisory along with Taxation & Assurance to name a few. Finsurety Advisors was incorporated in 2017 and has been in service for more than 4 years with clients ranging from sectors such as manufacturing, hospitality, infrastructure, startups, energy and technology. We aim to build an environment where ideas, innovations and solutions flow freely so as to add maximum value to our clients and all the stakeholders associated with the firm at various levels. The sister concern of Finsurety Advisors LLP is a Chartered Accountancy firm with more than 20 years of experience in various fields.


Our Partners




Mr Ravinder Khanduja

Mr Akash Khanduja

ACA
