About Our Team And Its Approach To Transaction Advisory


Transaction advisory requires a meticulous approach in its planning and execution. There are various facets which are covered under the umbrella of transaction advisory such as business development consulting, capital raising, joint venture negotiations, dealing with overseas litigation authorities, mergers and acquisition propositions, and so on.

At Coinmen, our transaction advisory team comprises of in-house finance experts who work with you to ensure the seamless planning, development and execution of all steps and stages and provide holistic consultancy which hold potential to scale up and explore synergies with suitable business partners and other firms.

Complementing the transaction advisory team is the constant involvement of our partners and our internal tax and regulatory services team, as this cross-functionality ensures an efficient and optimized transaction structure.

Additionally, our vast business network allows us access to lawyers, counsels, and high-profile industry professionals to identify as well as cover potential risks, and to get industry knowledge. As we implement the motto of pro-active involvement in our client’s business, our deep understanding of the industry allows us to dive deep into evaluating effective business propositions We also offer end-to-end transaction advisory services for overseas expansions which include analysis of overseas tax jurisdictions, annual tax compliance adherence, negotiations with foreign parties and profound business valuations.


Detailed List Of Functions Undertaken


We undertake the following functions under the ambit of transaction advisory services:

  • Management of sell-side mandates
  • Overseas acquisitions
  • Due diligence throughout the year
  • Business valuations
  • Negotiation support for transactions
  • Transaction and business structuring from tax, regulatory and funding perspectives
  • Post-transaction compliance support
  • Raising capital from Non-Banking Finance Companies
  • Privately negotiated transactions
  • Private placements and capital markets
  • Alternate financing


All our Transaction Advisory work is undertaken by our sister-concern Coinmen Capital Advisors LLP
