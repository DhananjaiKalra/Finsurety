Introducing Finsurety Advisors

Finsurety Advisors LLP which stands for ‘Financial Surety & Security’ for our clients is what we as a firm aim at achieving. Co-founded by two Chartered Accountants, Finsurety Advisors seeks to provide a one stop solution for clients looking for a diverse range of professional services. Along with our team of professionals from different domains we have managed to create a pool of individuals with rich experience in the fields of Debt Raising & Restructuring, Process Improvement, Risk Advisory along with Taxation & Assurance to name a few. Finsurety Advisors was incorporated in 2017 and has been in service for more than 4 years with clients ranging from sectors such as manufacturing, hospitality, infrastructure, startups, energy and technology. We aim to build an environment where ideas, innovations and solutions flow freely so as to add maximum value to our clients and all the stakeholders associated with the firm at various levels.


Our Services



Tax Advisory
Tax Compliance & Advisory – Direct & Indirect Tax
Along with our team of experienced tax consultants we provide tax advisory and regulatory services in both Direct & Indirect Tax that can help you minimise your tax exposure and highlight the risks presented by constantly evolving and increasingly complex legislation.


Audit and Assurance
Audit & Assurance
We understand that an audit is not merely comparing the numbers in books with documents to confirm their veracity hence with the help exceptional audit tools and procedures, and experienced staff, we pride ourselves that we deliver the highest quality auditing services.


Financial Accounting
Accounting Advisory & Bookkeeping
We possess the necessary expertise to provide cost-effective and high-quality financial accounting services. We help companies prepare for any new accounting changes and help implement them. This includes booking keeping and transition to latest reporting requirements.




Debt Structuring & Corporate Finance
Debt Structuring & Corporate Finance
Having successfully raised and restructured debts of over 800 crores, we provide our clients with an end to end solution for raising and structuring of different types of loans including project loans, equipment financing, SME financing etc.


Process Improvement & Risk Advisory
Process Improvement & Risk Advisory
We offer assessments and assistance that helps businesses improve their internal processes, streamline their organisational structure, drive cost reductions and improve quality of reporting and interdepartmental communication.


Secretarial Compliances
Secretarial Compliances & Incorporation
Along with our team of experts we assist and advise business owners / promoters / Non-Profits in incorporating the most suitable legal structure along with various statutory filings as required under various laws at every stage of their business lifecycle.
