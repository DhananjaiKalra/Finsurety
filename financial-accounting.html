Financial statements are the ultimate report card of a company’s fiscal performance. They enable owners and managers to pursue their business objectives, whether they are raising capital, doing acquisitions or planning divestment or restructuring. Therefore, every business activity, through the company’s lifecycle, impacts the financial statements and hence needs to be carefully examined and analysed at each step.

Our Specialised Financial Accounting Services

At Finsurety Advisors, we possess the necessary expertise and relevant resources to provide cost-effective and high-quality financial accounting services. We help companies prepare for any new accounting changes and help implement them.

Companies in India are transitioning to Ind AS in a phase wise manner hence there are bound to be reporting challenges for companies. Our team of professionals takes the pressure away from in-house finance teams by preparing a set of compliant financial statements.

We provide assistance on a wide variety of accounting processes, including but not limited to:
  • Bookkeeping services
  • Accounts receivable and payable
  • Payroll processing
  • Statutory compliances like TDS, GST, Advance Tax etc
  • Accounting supervision and controls.
  • Preparation of financial statements and annual reports
  • Helping in the transition to Ind AS & meeting reporting requirements
