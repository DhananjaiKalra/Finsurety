Various registrations under different acts are needed for organisations to conduct any type of activity in the country and certain compliances are also needed to be fulfilled for certain industries that an organisation might want to venture into. Along with this all the Companies, LLPs, Societies, Trusts, Non-Profits and other legal entities are required to statutorily report from time-to-time certain information under their respective governing Acts. The information to be reported ranges from Financial information like Revenue, Profits, Capital, Charge on Assets etc to Non-Financial Information like List of Directors, Shareholders, Registered Office of the Entity, Members of Society etc.

Finsurety’s Secretarial Compliances & Incorporation Services

At Finsurety Advisors, along with our team of professionals assist and advise business owners/promoters/Non-Profits in incorporating the most suitable legal structure so as to meet the needs of their business while keeping their compliance cost at the lowest. The right legal structure will always help the organisation make most of the opportunities that may arise from time to time. We also help organisations with various statutory filings as required under various laws at every stage of their business lifecycle. We help clients ensure timely reporting of accurate information and prepare the appropriate documentation as may be required to avoid unnecessary penalties and litigation.
