Indian Hospitality Sector

Over the past two decades, India has been a leading destination globally for outsourced processes of information technology development and allied services.

With new avenues opening up in the information technology industry in India and paving ways for pan-Indian connectivity, IT and ITeS make up for the majority in tech advancement. The country houses a large pool of new-age, young, technically skilled workforce which powers the sector from its grass-root level.

The Opportunity

The opportunity stems from the IT & ITeS and manufacturing industries in India, owed to a heavy demand in technical manpower outsourcing, digital infrastructure development, accessible technology in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities to promote Industry 4.0, and the need for emerging tech to propel India on to the world stage.
