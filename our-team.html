Our Team

The Pillars of the Firm




Mr Naveen Pruthi

ACA, LLB, MBA

Mr Naveen has over 8 years of experience in Internal Audit and indirect taxation . He has worked extensively in manufacturing, real estate, advertising and education sector. Before joining the team, Naveen worked with a Manufacturing Company as Accounts and Finance Executive for 2 years, as an Accounts and Finance Manager for a Real Estate & Construction company for 5 years and as an Associate with a Chartered Accountancy Firm for 2 years. He as an extensive understanding of the working of the industry and leads our Indirect Tax and Process Improvement & Risk Advisory Divisions. He has helped clients in structuring their businesses and helps them improve their efficiencies and work flows. He is also an expert in Risk Advisory and developing Management Information Systems for the clients.





Ms Juhi Aggarwal

ACA

Ms Juhi has post qualification experience of more than 3 years and leads the International Taxation have closely worked on taxation for Expats and advising clients in tax planning and representation before Tax & Other Authorities at different levels. She is well versed in International, Corporate and Personal Taxation. She also leads our Secretarial Compliances Divisions and handles all the filings under various Laws. Juhi also works closely in the Assurances & Financial Accounting domains having worked on clients from different industries. Over the years Juhi has also developed expertise in handling Accounting & Assurance for Government Agencies, Societies, Trusts & Companies.





Ms Nidhi Solanki

ACA

Ms Nidhi has over six years of experience focused on providing taxation, both direct and indirect, and auditing related services to the clients in various sectors such as medical, educational, hospitality, automotive sectors, and banking. She has worked closely on Forensic & Investigative Audits on many engagements. She has also assisted many private limited companies and LLPs in fulfilling their secretarial compliance requirements.





Ms Rakhi Gaur

B.Com, M.Com

Ms Rakhi is an Indirect Tax expert having deep knowledge of Goods & Service Tax. She represents clients before the Indirect Tax Authorities at various levels and has a keen eye for any changes in the Law that could benefit our clients. Along with Taxation, Rakhi handles Assurances for Technology Companies & Societies and Trusts.
